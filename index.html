About


Genre blending and versatility have always been key attributes of Dyluxe - the San Diego based OMNIA Nightclub/Hakkasan Group DJ and Dubspot Music School graduate, Dylan Talley. Over the course of the past year, we have seen the young talent rise, performing at top nightclubs and festivals around the world alongside names like Steve Aoki, The Chainsmokers, Carnage, Wiz Khalifa, Above & Beyond, Laidback Luke, DVBBS, Jauz, Slander, Nghtmre, Marshmello, and many more. Dyluxe premiered his debut original single “RAW” on ThisSongSlaps.com at the end of 2015. The track was shared over 650 times and went #1 on the Top 10. In Spring of 2016, Dyluxe went on an international tour, performing at festivals & nightclubs in Barcelona, Stockholm, Miami, Mexico, California & more. With his OMNIA residency, upcoming release on Trapstyle, and his Summer European Tour already in the works, Dyluxe is on track to do big things this year.
